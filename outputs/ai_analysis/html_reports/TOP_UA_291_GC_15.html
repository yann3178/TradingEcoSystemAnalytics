A Gold 15-minute breakout strategy that trades directional moves above/below session highs and lows. Uses multiple pattern filters to confirm trade direction and neutral market conditions before entering positions.

üéØ Conditions d'Entr√©e

LONG Entries: ‚Ä¢ Time window active (MyStartTrade to MyEndTrade) ‚Ä¢ Less than MaxTradesPerDay(4) entries today ‚Ä¢ PatternNeutralFast(16) = TRUE (neutral pattern confirmation) ‚Ä¢ PatternNeutralFast(30) = FALSE (avoid conflicting neutral pattern) ‚Ä¢ PatternDirectionalFast(+48) = TRUE (bullish directional pattern) ‚Ä¢ PatternDirectionalFast(+13) = FALSE (avoid conflicting directional pattern) ‚Ä¢ Buy at session high (myle) on stop SHORT Entries: ‚Ä¢ Same time and pattern neutral conditions as long ‚Ä¢ PatternDirectionalFast(-48) = TRUE (bearish directional pattern) ‚Ä¢ PatternDirectionalFast(-13) = FALSE (avoid conflicting directional pattern) ‚Ä¢ Sell short at session low (MySe) on stop

Normal Exits: ‚Ä¢ End of session exit when ID=0 and MyTrigger>=1 ‚Ä¢ SetExitOnClose when ID=0 and MyTrigger=0 ‚Ä¢ Time-based exit at MyEndTime Stop/Target Exits: ‚Ä¢ Stop loss: 2300 currency units ‚Ä¢ Take profit: 5000 currency units Time Exits: ‚Ä¢ DStop(432) bars since entry - force exit ‚Ä¢ Optional CloseAtTime(2500) override
