A session-based breakout strategy that trades breakouts of the previous bar's high/low with ADX momentum filter and pattern-based conditions. Uses time windows for entry control and operates on Natural Gas (NG) with 15-minute bars.

LONG Entries: ‚Ä¢ Previous bar's high + UpperLevelShift (-9 ticks) breakout ‚Ä¢ PatternNeutralFast(41) = true AND PatternNeutralFast(56) = false ‚Ä¢ PatternDirectionalFast(+52) = bullish directional pattern ‚Ä¢ ADX between 0-60 with cross above minimum ‚Ä¢ Time window: 0000-1000 or outside depending on configuration ‚Ä¢ Not trading month 4 (April) and not day 4 (Thursday) SHORT Entries: ‚Ä¢ Previous bar's low - LowerLevelShift (-6 ticks) breakout ‚Ä¢ PatternNeutralFast(41) = true AND PatternNeutralFast(56) = false ‚Ä¢ PatternDirectionalFast(-52) = bearish directional pattern ‚Ä¢ ADX between 0-60 with cross below maximum ‚Ä¢ Time window: 0000-2400 or outside depending on configuration ‚Ä¢ Not trading month 4 (April) and not day 0 (Sunday)
