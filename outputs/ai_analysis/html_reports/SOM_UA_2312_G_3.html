A session-based breakout strategy that enters long on breaks above N-session highs and short on breaks below N-session lows. Uses ADX filtering to avoid high volatility periods and multiple pattern confirmations to validate setups.

LONG Entries: ‚Ä¢ Break above N-session highest high (stop order) ‚Ä¢ Within trading window (MyStartTime to MyEndTime, excluding pause period) ‚Ä¢ ADX below threshold (ADXTH parameter) ‚Ä¢ All neutral patterns confirm (PtnNeutYes, PtnNeutYes2 true, PtnNeutNo false) ‚Ä¢ Directional patterns confirm (ptnDirYes true, ptnDirNo false for positive direction) ‚Ä¢ Not on excluded session day (SkipSessL) ‚Ä¢ OKL permission flag is true SHORT Entries: ‚Ä¢ Break below N-session lowest low (stop order) ‚Ä¢ Within trading window (MyStartTime to MyEndTime, excluding pause period) ‚Ä¢ ADX below threshold (ADXTH parameter) ‚Ä¢ All neutral patterns confirm (PtnNeutYes, PtnNeutYes2 true, PtnNeutNo false) ‚Ä¢ Directional patterns confirm (ptnDirYes true, ptnDirNo false for negative direction) ‚Ä¢ Not on excluded session day (SkipSessS) ‚Ä¢ OKS permission flag is true
