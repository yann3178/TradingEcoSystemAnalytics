A breakout strategy that trades various breakout levels (Donchian channels, session highs/lows, previous day levels) filtered by candlestick patterns. Uses pattern-based entry filters and stop-reversal logic for trend-following trades.

üéØ Conditions d'Entr√©e

LONG Entries: ‚Ä¢ Buy at breakout level (myle) when PatternFast(PtnLY=17) is TRUE ‚Ä¢ AND PatternFast(PtnLN=104) is FALSE (negative filter) ‚Ä¢ Only during specified time window ‚Ä¢ Maximum 1 trade per day ‚Ä¢ Avoid on specified weekday (MydayLongNo) SHORT Entries: ‚Ä¢ Sell short at breakdown level (MySe) when PatternFast(PtnSY=29) is TRUE ‚Ä¢ AND PatternFast(PtnSN=119) is FALSE (negative filter) ‚Ä¢ Same time and frequency restrictions as longs

üö™ Conditions de Sortie

Normal Exits: ‚Ä¢ Stop-reversal: Long positions stopped at MySe, Short positions at myle ‚Ä¢ Friday 16:55 market exit for all positions Stop/Target Exits: ‚Ä¢ Stop loss: MyStop (2200 currency units) ‚Ä¢ Take profit: MyProfit (0 - disabled) Time Exits: ‚Ä¢ End of session exit based on ID parameter ‚Ä¢ Optional bar count exit (uscitatotbarre)

PatternFast(17): body5d > 0.5 * (highd5-lowd1) ‚Ä¢ Meaning: 5-day body size exceeds 50% of 5-day to 1-day range ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Strong directional movement over 5 days ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Long entry confirmation PatternFast(104): lowd0 > (lowd1 + lowd1*2*0.01) ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Today's low is 2% higher than yesterday's low ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Strong support, no lower lows ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Negative filter for longs (avoid when true) PatternFast(29): body5d > 0.5 * range5d ‚Ä¢ Meaning: 5-day body exceeds 50% of 5-day range ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Strong trending movement in 5-day period ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Short entry confirmation PatternFast(119): opend0 < (closed1 - closed1*0.25*0.01) ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Today's open is 0.25% below yesterday's close ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Gap down opening ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Negative filter for shorts (avoid when true)
