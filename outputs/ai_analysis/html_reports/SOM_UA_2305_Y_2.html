A breakout strategy that trades breakouts of dynamic levels (highest/lowest of N periods) with multiple pattern filters and Williams indicator confirmation from a higher timeframe (30-minute data2). Uses complex filtering with up to 6 different pattern conditions that must align for trade entry.
