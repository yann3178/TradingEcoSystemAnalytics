This is a dual-timeframe breakout strategy that uses different session ranges to identify breakout levels. It goes long when price breaks above the short-term session low (when it's above the long-term low) and short when price breaks below the short-term session high (when it's below the long-term high). Pattern confirmation is required for both entries.
