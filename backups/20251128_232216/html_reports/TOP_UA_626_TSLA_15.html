üìù Summary

A volatility breakout strategy that enters long positions when price exceeds the previous close plus one standard deviation, and short positions when price falls below the previous close minus one standard deviation. Uses asymmetric lookback periods (60 for long, 200 for short) and includes day-of-week filtering.
