üìù Summary

A session-based breakout strategy that trades breakouts of the current session's high/low, but only when the previous day's close was positioned in the favorable 20% of its range. Uses currency-based stops and targets with day-of-week filtering.

üéØ Entry Conditions

LONG Entries: ‚Ä¢ Previous day's close > (low + 20% of range) - upper 20% filter ‚Ä¢ Current time between 00:00 and 15:00 ‚Ä¢ Day of week ‚â† DaytoFilterLng (0 = Sunday) ‚Ä¢ Buy at current session high breakout SHORT Entries: ‚Ä¢ Previous day's close < (high - 20% of range) - lower 20% filter ‚Ä¢ Current time between 00:00 and 15:00 ‚Ä¢ Day of week ‚â† DaytoFilterSht (5 = Friday) ‚Ä¢ Sell short at current session low breakdown
