üìù Summary

A breakout strategy that uses pattern recognition to trade above/below session highs and lows. Uses PatternFast conditions as entry filters and implements comprehensive risk management with time-based exits and breakeven protection.

üéØ Entry Conditions

LONG Entries: ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(32) = TRUE (upper shadow comparison pattern) ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(11) = FALSE (5-day body filter) ‚Ä¢ Buy at session high breakout (Highd(0) stop order) ‚Ä¢ Within trading hours (0830-1305, excluding pause 1200-1100) ‚Ä¢ Maximum 1 trade per day SHORT Entries: ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(39) = TRUE (upper shadow comparison) ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(135) = FALSE (outside bar filter) ‚Ä¢ Sell short at session low breakdown (Lowd(0) stop order) ‚Ä¢ Same time and frequency restrictions as longs

üîç Pattern Details

PatternFast(32): ((highd0-opend0)> ((highd1-opend1)*0.5)) ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Today's upper shadow > 50% of yesterday's upper shadow ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Increased buying pressure or rejection at higher levels ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Long entry filter PatternFast(39): ((highd0-opend0)< ((highd1-opend1))) ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Today's upper shadow < yesterday's upper shadow ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Less rejection at highs, potential for continuation ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Short entry filter PatternFast(11): body5d < 0.5 * range5d ‚Ä¢ Meaning: 5-day body is less than 50% of 5-day range ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Consolidation period, indecision ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Must be FALSE for long entries (avoiding consolidation) PatternFast(135): (highd0>highd1 and lowd0<lowd1) ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Today's high > yesterday's high AND today's low < yesterday's low ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Outside bar, increased volatility ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Must be FALSE for short entries (avoiding volatile conditions)
