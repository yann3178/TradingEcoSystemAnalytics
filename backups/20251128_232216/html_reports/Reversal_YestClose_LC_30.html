üìù Summary

This is a reversal strategy that trades against the previous session's close direction. It enters long when yesterday closed down (red bar) by buying at yesterday's low plus a percentage buffer, and enters short when yesterday closed up (green bar) by selling at yesterday's high minus a percentage buffer.
