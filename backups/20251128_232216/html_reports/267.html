üìù Summary

This is a trend following strategy using channel breakouts with sophisticated pattern filtering. It trades both long and short based on breakouts of 1-bar high/low channels, but only when specific market patterns are present and absent.

üéØ Entry Conditions

LONG Entries: ‚Ä¢ Buy next bar at upperchannel (highest high of 1 bar) stop ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(64) must be TRUE: highd0 < (lowd0 + lowd0*2*0.01) - indicating constrained daily range ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(116) must be FALSE: NOT (opend0 < lowd1 OR opend0 > highd1) - current open must be within yesterday's range ‚Ä¢ Within time window (18:00-17:00) excluding pause (12:00-11:00) ‚Ä¢ Maximum 2 entries per day SHORT Entries: ‚Ä¢ Sell short next bar at lowerchannel (lowest low of 1 bar) stop ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(54) must be TRUE: highd0 > (lowd0 + lowd0*0.5*0.01) - indicating minimum daily range expansion ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(50) must be FALSE: NOT (closed1<closed2<closed3<closed4<closed5) - avoiding strong downtrends ‚Ä¢ Same time and frequency restrictions as longs

üîç Pattern Details

PatternFast(64): highd0 < (lowd0 + lowd0*2*0.01) ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Today's high is less than today's low plus 2% of low ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Very constrained daily range, low volatility ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Long entry filter for breakout setup PatternFast(116): opend0 < lowd1 OR opend0 > highd1 ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Today's open is outside yesterday's range (gap) ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Gap up or gap down scenario ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Long entry filter (FALSE = no gap required) PatternFast(54): highd0 > (lowd0 + lowd0*0.5*0.01) ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Today's high exceeds low plus 0.5% of low ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Minimum daily range expansion ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Short entry filter for volatility PatternFast(50): closed1<closed2<closed3<closed4<closed5 ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Five consecutive declining closes ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Strong bearish momentum ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Short entry filter (FALSE = avoid strong downtrends)
