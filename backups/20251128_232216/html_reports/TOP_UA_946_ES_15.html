üìù Summary

Long-only reversal strategy that enters at a specific time (17:15) when the market has gapped down from the previous day's open within a defined range. Uses pattern confirmation and volatility filtering to identify reversal opportunities after bearish gaps.

üéØ Entry Conditions

LONG Entries: ‚Ä¢ Entry at 17:15 (specific time) ‚Ä¢ Previous close < previous open (gap down condition) ‚Ä¢ Gap size between 0.05% and 2.5% of previous open ‚Ä¢ Current TrueRange > 0.7x AvgTrueRange(6) (volatility filter) ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(152) = TRUE (always true pattern) ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(121) = FALSE (current open NOT < previous close by 1%) ‚Ä¢ Current low < previous low (lower low condition) ‚Ä¢ Skip specified day of week (skipday parameter) SHORT Entries: ‚Ä¢ None - long-only strategy
