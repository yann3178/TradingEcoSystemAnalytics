üìù Summary

This strategy trades mean reversion signals using Bollinger Band extremes while filtering trades based on Keltner Channel positioning on a secondary timeframe (data2). It goes long when price touches the lower Bollinger Band and short when touching the upper band, but only when certain market bias conditions are met.
