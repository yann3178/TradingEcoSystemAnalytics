üìù Summary

This is a Donchian Channel breakout strategy that enters long/short positions when price breaks above/below the highest high or lowest low of the last 46 bars. The strategy uses multiple pattern filters to qualify entries and includes session-based trading with comprehensive risk management.

üéØ Entry Conditions

LONG Entries: ‚Ä¢ Buy next bar at UpperDonchianNBars (46-bar high) stop ‚Ä¢ PatternNeutralFast(26) = True AND PatternNeutralFast(9) = False AND PatternNeutralFast(56) = False ‚Ä¢ PatternDirectionalFast(+52) = True AND PatternDirectionalFast(+35) = False ‚Ä¢ Within trading time window and daily volatility filter ‚Ä¢ Max 1 trade per day limit not exceeded SHORT Entries: ‚Ä¢ Sell short next bar at LowerDonchianNBars (46-bar low) stop ‚Ä¢ Same neutral pattern conditions as long ‚Ä¢ PatternDirectionalFast(-52) = True AND PatternDirectionalFast(-35) = False ‚Ä¢ Same time and risk filters as long

üö™ Exit Conditions

Normal Exits: ‚Ä¢ Christmas holiday exit (Dec 23 - Jan 6) ‚Ä¢ Friday end-of-week close Stop/Target Exits: ‚Ä¢ Stop loss at 3000 currency units ‚Ä¢ Take profit at 5500 currency units ‚Ä¢ Breakeven at 2750 currency units Time Exits: ‚Ä¢ Optional close at specified time (CloseAtTime input)

üîç Pattern Details

PatternNeutralFast(26): body5d < 0.75 * range5d ‚Ä¢ Meaning: 5-day body less than 75% of 5-day range ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Moderate consolidation with some wicks ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Allows for breakout setup with reasonable volatility PatternNeutralFast(9): body5d < 0.1 * (highd5-lowd1) ‚Ä¢ Meaning: 5-day body very small compared to price span ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Extreme consolidation ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Filtered out (False condition) - avoids too tight ranges PatternNeutralFast(56): (highd0>(lowd0+lowd0*1.5*0.01)) ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Current high > low + 1.5% of low ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Minimum daily range requirement ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Filtered out (False) - ensures adequate volatility PatternDirectionalFast(52): highd1<highd2 and lowd1<lowd2 ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Previous day completely inside day before ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Contraction/inside day pattern ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Breakout setup after compression PatternDirectionalFast(35): (highd0-opend0)> (highd1-opend1)*1.5 ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Today's upper wick 1.5x larger than yesterday's ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Excessive upper rejection ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Filtered out (False) - avoids failed breakout patterns
