üìù Summary

This is a session-based breakout strategy that trades breakouts above the previous session's high or below the previous session's low. The strategy uses multiple pattern filters to confirm entries and avoid false breakouts, operating during specific trading hours with comprehensive risk management.

LONG Entries: ‚Ä¢ Pattern filter MyPtnLY(65) must be TRUE ‚Ä¢ Pattern filter MyPtnLN(47) must be FALSE (negation) ‚Ä¢ Buy next bar at previous session's high (highd1) on stop ‚Ä¢ Must be within trading window (03:30 to 17:00) SHORT Entries: ‚Ä¢ Pattern filter MyPtnSY(132) must be TRUE ‚Ä¢ Pattern filter MyPtnSN(82) must be FALSE (negation) ‚Ä¢ Sell short next bar at previous session's low (lowd1) on stop ‚Ä¢ Must be within trading window (03:30 to 17:00)
