üìù Summary

A time-based bias strategy that determines long/short bias at specific session times using pattern conditions, then executes trades via breakout or pullback methods within defined time windows. The strategy uses session OHLC data and pattern recognition to filter entry conditions.

LONG Entries: ‚Ä¢ At bar MyLEBar (16): PatternFast(MyPtnLY=15) TRUE and PatternFast(MyPtnLN=44) FALSE ‚Ä¢ Day of week cannot be MyNotLEDay (3=Wednesday) ‚Ä¢ Entry Type 1: Market order at bias time ‚Ä¢ Entry Type 2: Breakout at highest(H,3) during window bars 16-8 ‚Ä¢ Entry Type 3: Pullback at lowest(L,1) during window bars 16-8 SHORT Entries: ‚Ä¢ At bar MySEBar (8): PatternFast(MyPtnSY=70) TRUE and PatternFast(MyPtnSN=114) FALSE ‚Ä¢ Day of week cannot be MyNotSEDay (3=Wednesday) ‚Ä¢ Entry Type 1: Market order at bias time ‚Ä¢ Entry Type 2: Breakout at lowest(L,1) during window bars 8-16 ‚Ä¢ Entry Type 3: Pullback at highest(H,3) during window bars 8-16

üîç Pattern Details
