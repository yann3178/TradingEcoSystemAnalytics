üìù Summary

Session breakout strategy that trades breaks above/below previous session high/low levels. Uses pattern filtering to confirm entries and includes a midday pause to avoid choppy market periods.

üéØ Entry Conditions

LONG Entries: ‚Ä¢ Time between 10:00-18:00 AND outside pause window (13:00-15:00) ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(3) = TRUE: Small body (< 50% of range) ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(113) = FALSE: NOT (downtrend + gap up) ‚Ä¢ Buy at previous session high on stop SHORT Entries: ‚Ä¢ Time between 10:00-18:00 AND outside pause window (13:00-15:00) ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(94) = TRUE: Recent high < 5-day high ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(69) = FALSE: NOT bearish close ‚Ä¢ Sell at previous session low on stop

üîç Pattern Details

PatternFast(3): body1d < 0.5 * range1d ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Previous day's body is less than 50% of its range ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Indecision, potential breakout setup ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Long entry filter for consolidation PatternFast(94): highd1 < highd5 ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Yesterday's high is below 5-day high ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Recent weakness, pullback from highs ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Short entry filter for weakness PatternFast(113): closed1<closed2 and closed2<closed3 and opend0<closed1 ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Two declining closes followed by gap down open ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Downtrend with gap down ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Long filter (must be FALSE - no downtrend gap) PatternFast(69): closed1 < opend1 ‚Ä¢ Meaning: Previous day closed below its open (bearish) ‚Ä¢ Indicates: Bearish sentiment ‚Ä¢ Trading use: Short filter (must be FALSE - no bearish close)

vars: highd0 (0), lowd0 (0), IsStartOfSession(true);

input: sessionStartTimeC(800),sessionEndTimeC(2200);
Input: MyPtnLY(3), MyPtnSY(94), MyPtnLN(113), MyPtnSN(69);
Input: MyStartTrade(1000), MyEndTrade(1800), MyStartPause(1300), MyEndPause(1500), ID(0);
Input: MyStop(2500), MyProfit(7500), Randomvalue(0);

IsStartOfSession = _OHLCMulti5Rev2(sessionStartTimeC, sessionEndTimeC, ohlcValues, Randomvalue);
highd0 = ohlcvalues[1]; lowd0 = ohlcvalues[2]; //these are picked depending on the chosen trigger, in this example high and low of last session

//here the patterns are picked (presi) for each direction in an independent way so that the whole setup can be unmirrored
//in fact the general function Patternfast is used
If tw(MyStartTrade,MyEndTrade) and (Time < MyStartPause or time > MyEndPause) then begin
	If PatternFast(MyPtnLY, ohlcValues) and PatternFast(MyPtnLN, ohlcValues)=False then buy MyContracts contracts next bar at highd0 stop;
	If PatternFast(MyPtnSY, ohlcValues) and PatternFast(MyPtnSN, ohlcValues)=False then sellshort MyContracts contracts next bar at lowd0 stop;
end;

Setstopcontract;
If MyStop > 0 then setstoploss(MyStop);
If MyProfit > 0 then setprofittarget(MyProfit);

if ID=0 then setexitonclose;
