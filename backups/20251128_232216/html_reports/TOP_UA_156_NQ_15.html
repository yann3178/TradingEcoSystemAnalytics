üìù Summary

An unmirrored trend following breakout strategy that trades NQ futures on 15-minute bars. Uses independent pattern filtering for long and short setups, breaking above/below previous session highs/lows during specified trading hours.

üéØ Entry Conditions

LONG Entries: ‚Ä¢ Time window: 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM (tw function) ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(54) = TRUE: Daily range > 0.5% of low price ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(75) = FALSE: Close NOT down 2.5% from previous close ‚Ä¢ Entry: Buy stop at previous session high (highd1) SHORT Entries: ‚Ä¢ Time window: 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(111) = TRUE: Previous day low > lows of 3 days before ‚Ä¢ PatternFast(31) = FALSE: Current high NOT 25% greater than previous high ‚Ä¢ Entry: Sell short stop at previous session low (lowd1)

üîç Pattern Details
