üìù Summary

A long-only breakout strategy that buys above the previous session's high (0800-2200) with multiple pattern and compression filters. Uses ADX filtering to avoid trending markets and includes day-of-week filtering.

üéØ Entry Conditions

LONG Entries: ‚Ä¢ Time window: After starttrade(800) and before endtrade(2000) ‚Ä¢ PatternNeutralFast(55) = TRUE AND PatternNeutralFast(56) = FALSE ‚Ä¢ ADX(5) on data2 < 60 (low trend strength) ‚Ä¢ Compression filter: Current open vs previous close within trigger range ‚Ä¢ Day filter: mydow ‚â† skipdayL(1) (skip Mondays) ‚Ä¢ PatternDirectionalFast(27) = TRUE ‚Ä¢ PtnBaseSA2(41) = TRUE AND PtnBaseSA2(29) = FALSE ‚Ä¢ Buy at previous session high minus 2 points on stop order
